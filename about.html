My name is Sukhpreet Singh.

I am a commercial photographer and art director based in New Delhi.

I help brands and individuals stand out in the marketplace through bespoke imagery.

My love for photography began at a very young age, when I was 14 years old.

I did not have many friends, as I didn’t speak a word of English at first. My first camera

was a way I made friends. I fell in love with the process and the result, creating many

connections along the way (even with my broken English). To this day, cultivating warm

client relationships, delivering work that is consistent and unique to each client, remains my philosophy.
